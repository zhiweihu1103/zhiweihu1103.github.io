Short Bio

I am now a fourth-year PhD student (2020-) in Computer Science at Shanxi University, supervised by Prof. Ru Li, Prof. Jeff Z. Pan and Prof. Víctor Gutiérrez Basulto. My research interests focus on representation learning for knowledge graph, including graph representation learning, multi-hop logical reasoning, also I am interested in knowledge distillation in LLMs.
